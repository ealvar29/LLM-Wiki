# Space Marines

![Space Marines](https://www.warhammer-community.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/pTVHKAjRk2B0JWCO.jpg)

Space Marines (Adeptus Astartes) are the signature faction of Warhammer 40,000 — genetically engineered superhuman warriors created to defend the Imperium. They are the most popular and widely collected army in the game.

## Playstyle

Space Marines are a versatile, forgiving army — good for beginners but deep enough for competitive play. They excel at:

- **Mid-range shooting** — bolters, plasma, and melta at 18–24"
- **Durable infantry** — 2 wounds per Marine, 3+ saves baseline
- **Flexible detachments** — one of the largest detachment pools in the game
- **Vehicle synergy** — some of the best tanks and transports in 40K

## 10th Edition Overview

In 10th edition Space Marines have access to a huge variety of **Chapter Tactics** via detachments. Each detachment changes the army's identity significantly — from elite close combat (Black Templars) to deep strike shooting (Raven Guard) to vehicle-heavy armoured assaults.

### Standout Units

| Unit | Role | Why It's Good |
|---|---|---|
| Infernus Marines | Shooting | Devastating Wounds on flamers wrecks hordes |
| Brutalis Dreadnought | Close Combat | Extreme melee damage output |
| Repulsor Executioner | Anti-tank | Heavy laser destroyer hits hard at range |
| Sternguard Veterans | Shooting | Special issue boltguns with high AP |
| Lieutenant | Support | Re-roll 1s to wound — fits every list |

## Current Detachments (10th Ed)

- **Gladius Task Force** — the default; re-roll 1s to hit in Tactical Doctrine
- **Ironstorm Spearhead** — vehicle-focused; free stratagems for tanks
- **Stormlance Task Force** — fast cavalry and bikes
- **Firestorm Assault Force** — flamers and aggressive infantry
- **Anvil Siege Force** — long-range devastation, sits back and shoots
- **Vanguard Spearhead** — Phobos infiltration and scouting
- **Headhunter Task Force** *(Armageddon)* — Tank Aces, vehicle character upgrades
- **Armoured Speartip** *(Armageddon)* — Heavy Transports with Rapid Deployment
