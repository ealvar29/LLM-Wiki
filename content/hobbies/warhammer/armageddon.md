# Armageddon: The Return of Yarrick

Current major Warhammer 40,000 supplement (April 2026). Focused on the legendary war world of Armageddon with new vehicle-centric rules, campaign missions, and faction detachments.

## Armoured Gauntlet — New Vehicle Rules

The headline mechanic: **SPEARHEAD** keyword for large tanks and monsters (10+ Wounds). Instead of dying when they hit 0 wounds, Spearhead units roll on damage tables for cinematic results — engine fires, crew casualties, reduced movement — before eventually being destroyed.

Different unit types have different tables:
- **Transports** — cargo access, movement reduction
- **Monsters** — rampage states, reduced WS/BS
- **Vehicles** — engine damage, weapons knocked out

**Spearhead Upgrades** let veteran units earn special abilities across campaigns:
- Space Marine Dreadnoughts can become Venerable Ancients
- Ork vehicles get Kustom Mek Jobs

## New Space Marine Detachments

### Headhunter Task Force

Tank-focused detachment built around **Tank Aces** — up to 3 vehicles become Ace units with CHARACTER status, granting access to enhancements and Warlord selection.

Tank Aces choose one ability per turn:
- Add **6" movement** when advancing
- **Re-roll damage rolls** if they didn't advance

Key stratagems:
- **Kill Shot** — anti-vehicle precision
- **Target Weak Point** — AP improvement

### Armoured Speartip

Transport-assault detachment. Vehicles with 14+ wounds become **HEAVY TRANSPORT** units.

Core mechanic — **Rapid Deployment**: units disembarking gain bonus movement:
- Normal transports: **D6"** extra
- Heavy Transports: **D3+3"** extra (more reliable)

**Liberator enhancement**: Characters (or their transports) can hold objectives without dismounting — massive for objective games.

## Campaign Rules

12 missions with:
- Balanced competitive play scenarios
- Asymmetrical challenge missions
- Persistent upgrade progression across battles (units improve over a campaign)

## Affected Factions

Core Spearhead rules apply to all factions. New detachments added for:
- Space Marines
- Orks
- Astra Militarum
