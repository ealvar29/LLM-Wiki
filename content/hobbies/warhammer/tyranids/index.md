# Tyranids

![Tyranid Swarm](https://www.warhammer-community.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/Jkz85I9Gl1vwjNYb.jpg)

Tyranids are an extragalactic alien swarm driven by a single purpose: consuming all biological matter in the universe to fuel the Hive Mind. One of the most visually striking and thematically unique factions in 40K — an army of monsters, swarms, and bio-engineered horrors.

## Playstyle

Tyranids are a horde-and-monster army with a unique mechanic: **Synapse**. Units within Synapse range of a psyker-like Synapse creature are buffed and more reliable. Stray too far from Synapse and your swarms become unpredictable.

They excel at:

- **Overwhelming numbers** — cheap Termagants and Hormagaunts drown opponents in bodies
- **Monster mash** — Carnifexes, Hive Tyrants, and Screamer-Killers hit extremely hard in melee
- **Objective denial** — fast, numerous units can swamp objectives
- **Adaptation** — multiple detachments for different playstyles (swarm vs big bugs)

## 10th Edition Overview

10th edition was a strong showing for Tyranids. The **Invasion Fleet** detachment is the default and one of the best in the faction — it rewards aggressive play with Synaptic Imperatives that cycle each turn.

### Standout Units

| Unit | Role | Why It's Good |
|---|---|---|
| Screamer-Killer | Monster / CC | Devastating Wounds on Bio-plasmic Scream, terrifying melee |
| Neurolictor | Skirmish | Scout + disrupts opponent's command phase abilities |
| Termagants | Troops | Cheap, numerous, excellent with Fleshborer re-rolls |
| Hive Tyrant | HQ | Flexible; good in melee or shooting, unlocks Warlord traits |
| Winged Hive Tyrant | HQ | Highly mobile version; one of the best HQs in the game |
| Neurotyrant | Support | Boosts Synapse, huge force multiplier |
| Von Ryan's Leapers | Fast Attack | Fast, hit hard, good for threatening backlines |

## Current Detachments (10th Ed)

- **Invasion Fleet** — default; Synaptic Imperatives cycle turn-by-turn buffs
- **Crusher Stampede** — all-monster list; extra hits on charge
- **Vanguard Onslaught** — infiltrators and scouts; Neurolictor synergies
- **Assimilation Swarm** — objective control and resilience
- **Synaptic Nexus** — Synapse-heavy; powerful psychic-adjacent abilities

## The Lore

Tyranids enter 40K through **Hive Fleets** — massive living armadas that strip entire star systems bare. Major hive fleets include:

- **Behemoth** — first major invasion; stopped at Macragge by the Ultramarines
- **Kraken** — shattered into countless splinter fleets; harder to stop
- **Leviathan** — largest yet; emerged from below the galactic plane, bypassing Imperial defenses entirely
