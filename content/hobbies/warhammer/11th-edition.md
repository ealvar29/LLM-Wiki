# 11th Edition — What We Know

> This page is a live tracker. It will be updated as announcements are made.

## Current Status

As of April 2026, **no official announcement** has been made by Games Workshop. We are still in **10th Edition** (launched June 2023).

Games Workshop typically runs editions for 3–4 years. Based on that pattern, 11th Edition could plausibly be announced in late 2026 or 2027 — but nothing is confirmed.

## What to Watch

When announcements come, they typically follow this sequence:

1. **Teaser images** on Warhammer Community (social media first)
2. **"New Edition" reveal article** with a boxed starter set
3. **Rules previews** — usually weeks before launch
4. **Launch box** containing two starter forces

The Warhammer Community website and their social channels are the authoritative source. Leaks often surface on Reddit (r/Warhammer40k) and Dakka Dakka before official reveals.

## Speculation & Community Expectations

Based on community discussion heading into 2026, common expectations for 11th edition include:

- Further rules streamlining (10th was already simpler than 9th)
- New starter box factions (the starter usually introduces or refreshes key armies)
- Possible changes to the Detachment system
- More frequent points updates via the app
