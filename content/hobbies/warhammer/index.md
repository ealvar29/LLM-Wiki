# Warhammer 40,000

Warhammer 40,000 (40K) is a tabletop miniature wargame set in a dark science-fantasy universe 38,000 years in the future. Made by Games Workshop, it combines collecting and painting miniatures with tactical gameplay.

## Current Edition

**11th Edition** — announced AdeptiCon March 2026, releasing **Summer 2026**. Launch box: Space Marines vs. Orks (Armageddon).
